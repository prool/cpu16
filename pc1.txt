Personal Computer "Prool PC-1"

processor Prool-16
RAM 64K

address space:

total RAM: 0000 - F81F

F820-F82F - I/O registers:

F820-F821 - keyboard register (high byte - status, low byte - inputting character)

screen memory: F830 - FFFF (25 lines, 80 columns, 1 byte - 1 symbol, without color or attributes)
